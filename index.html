Copenhagen NLP Symposium
Friday, 20 June, 2025
Arbejdermuseet, Copenhagen, Denmark
Register


The symposium is centred around highlighting recent research from NLP researchers in Denmark and beyond, inviting international researchers to provide inspiring keynotes, and providing scaffolding and time for networking across academia, industry, and students.

Keynote Speakers

Loubna ben Allal is a Research Engineer at HuggingFace.

Marzieh Fadaee is a Staff Research Scientist at Cohere Labs.

Najoung Kim is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Linguistics and an Affiliated Faculty at the Department of Computer Science at Boston University.

Kyle Lo research scientist at the Allen Institute for AI.

Yohei Oseki is an Associate Professor at the University of Tokyo.

Call for Posters

We invite researchers to submit a request to present a poster on any topic related to Natural Language Processing. The registration request form contains a dedicated section for this information. You should include a title and abstract for your poster, and, where possible, information about whether the work is previously published.
